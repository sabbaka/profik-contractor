Profik Pro is the app for cleaning professionals who want a steady stream of local jobs — without cold calls, ads, or chasing leads.

Browse open jobs near you, send your price, and win work directly from customers.

— HOW IT WORKS —

1. Browse open jobs
See new cleaning jobs on a map and in a list — service type, property details, location, and the client's budget up front.

2. Send your offer
Set your price and add a short intro so the client knows why you're the right pick.

3. Chat with clients
Agree on details in the built-in chat — no need to share your phone number.

4. Get the job done
Track your jobs from accepted to completed and collect real reviews that win you more work.

— WHY PROFIK PRO —

• New local jobs with full details and budgets shown up front
• You set your own price on every offer
• Built-in chat with clients
• Push notifications for new messages and offer updates
• Ratings and reviews that build your reputation
• Simple in-app balance for platform fees

— WHO IT'S FOR —

Independent cleaners, cleaning teams, and small cleaning businesses looking for standard, deep, and post-renovation cleaning jobs in their area.
